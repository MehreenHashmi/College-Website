SPORTS

    BVCOE, New Delhi always emphasized on the overall development of the students. In this direction, Annual Sports meet of the college is organized every year in which Students of all branches actively participate. It includes indoor and as well as outdoor Events (like Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Badminton, Athletics, Table Tennis, Carom etc). Winners of the respective events are facilitated with Medals and Trophies.
